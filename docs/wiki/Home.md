## Overview

Raptor HR helps organizations manage:

- **Employee Directory** - Centralized employee profiles and contact information
- **Organization Structure** - Departments, positions, and reporting relationships
- **Leave Management** - PTO requests, approvals, and balance tracking
- **Time Tracking** - Daily time entries and timesheet submissions
- **Contracts** - Employment contract management and document storage
